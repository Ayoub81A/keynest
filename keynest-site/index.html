Features

Everything you need to stay secure

KeyNest is designed to be simple enough to use every day, and secure enough to trust with your most sensitive data.

End-to-End Encryption
Passwords are encrypted in your browser with AES-256-GCM before they ever reach the server. The key never leaves your device.
Password Generator
Generate strong, random passwords up to 40 characters with full control over character sets — uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
Health Report
Get a vault health score and see all weak, reused, and medium-strength passwords in one place with direct links to fix them.
Secure Notes
Store sensitive information beyond passwords — WiFi credentials, license keys, PINs, or anything else you need kept private.
Tags & Organisation
Organise entries with custom tags. Filter by tag instantly from the sidebar. Assign multiple tags per entry.
Trash & Restore
Accidentally deleted something? Entries go to trash first and are kept for 30 days before permanent deletion. Restore with one click.
How It Works

Simple. Secure. Private.

Three steps to a more secure digital life.

1

Create an account

Register with your email and a strong master password. Your password is hashed with bcrypt — we never store it in plain text.

2

Add your passwords

Each entry is encrypted in your browser with AES-256-GCM before being stored. The server receives only ciphertext it can never read.

3

Access anywhere

Log in from any device running KeyNest. Your encryption key is derived from your password on the fly and wiped on logout.
